LiTHe kod's Amazing Coding Challenges (LACC)

What is LACC?

LACC is a series of small coding challenges that are very loosely defined. The rules are simple: make stuff, then share it. The focus is on creating something and being creative, and that doesn't always translate into a "pleasing" end result.

Think of it as creative writing prompts, but for programming. How it's made, doesn't matter. How it's working, doesn't matter. The theme is revealed around 17:00 Swedish time each Tuesday, and just like in kindergarten everyone is a winner.

To join, simply post your output in the LiTHe kod "LACC" slack-channel . And just like LiU's exams, everything is allowed, except stealing other people's work.
